WOOTLAB TRAINEE INFORMATION

Wootlab Foundation is a non-profit organization that leverages technology to promote inclusive and quality education for out-of-school children and youths in Africa, thereby advancing sustainable development goals in promoting employability and decent work for economic growth.

Jobberman is West Africa's leading employment board, supporting employers in filling openings swiftly and cost-effectively by connecting them with the most suitable candidates. As the custodians of over 2.5 million job seekers on our platform, we are accountable for developing solutions that better prepare young people for work.

In an effort to bridge the digital skill gap in Nigeria, Wootlab Foundation, in conjunction with Jobberman, is training individuals in fundamental digital skills. This project aims to up-skill young people ensuring their skillset match with opportunities in the digital space.
